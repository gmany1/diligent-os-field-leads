# Data Retention Policy

## 1. Active Data
*   **Leads**: Retained indefinitely while `stage` is NOT `WON` or `LOST`.
*   **Active Users**: Retained while employed.

## 2. Inactive Data
*   **Lost Leads**: Retained for 18 months for re-marketing, then candidates for Soft Delete.
*   **Won Leads**: Retained for 7 years (Finance/Tax compliance).
*   **Inactive Users**: Soft deleted immediately upon termination; Hard deleted after 1 year.

## 3. Audit Logs
*   **Retention Period**: 1 year online, 5 years archived (cold storage).
*   **Purpose**: Security, Compliance, CCPA verification.

## 4. CCPA Requests
*   **Deletion**: Must be processed within 45 days of verification.
*   **Export**: Must be provided within 45 days.
